ABOUT US

The Diaspora World Cup (DWC) is an annual international soccer tournament.
DWC is hosted in Ottawa, Canada.
The tournament consists of 32 teams/countries of adult men who are unofficial representatives of their countries of origin.
The tournament lasts 6 weeks and the champions win $7 500.
