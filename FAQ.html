FAQ

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions


Match Rules


  1. Is the offside rule in effect during the matches?

    2. Answer: No, DWC matches do not enforce the offside rule.


  2. Are cleats or studs allowed during the matches?

    3. Answer: No, soccer players are not allowed to wear studs or cleats for saftey reasons.


  3. Is it possible for a soccer player to be eliminated from the tournament?

    4. Answer: Yes, only if a soccer player initates a physical attack on another person during the tournament.


Match Structure


  1. What happens if a match results in a draw? Is there extra time?

    2. Answer: During the group stage, matches can end in draws and each team receives 1 point. During the knock out stages, there must be a tie-breaker so matches proceed directly to penalties. There is no extra time.


  2. How many players are there per team?

    3. Answer: Each teams consists of 15 players (11 starters and 4 substitutes). Each team is free to decide their starting line up and make unlimited substitutions so that everyone plays every match.


  3. How long do the matches last?

    4. Answer: Each match lasts 50 minutes. There are two 25-minute halves separated by a 10-minute break.


Tournament


  1. Is there a match for the 3rd place?

    2. Answer: No, there is no match for the 3rd place. The losers of the semi finals are eliminated from the tournament.


  2. Do the champions win a prize?

    3. Answer: Yes, the winning team wins $7 500 CAD.


Countries


  1. Why is my country not competing in the tournament?

    2. Answer: Only 32 teams (31 of which are countries) compete every year, that means 162 member states of the United Nations will not be able to participate every year.


  2. Can I still play for a team if my country of origin is not competing in tournament this year?

    3. Answer: Yes, one team (United Nations) is reserved for people of all backgrounds.


Prices


  1. How much are the tickets?

    2. Answer: The tickets to watch the games are $15 CAD + tax.


  2. How much is it to represent my country as a soccer player?

    3. Answer: The sign up price for soccer players is $125 CAD + tax.
