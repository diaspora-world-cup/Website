International Soccer Tournament

Ottawa, Canada

TEAM STANDINGS

The tournament consists of 2 major stages: the group stage followed by the knock-out stage.

During the group stage, the 32 teams are divided into 8 groups of 4 teams.
Each group plays a total of 6 matches to determine the best 2 out of 4 teams of each group.

During the knock-out stage, a match loss means elimination form the tournament.
The survivors of the group stage move through to the round-of-16, then to the quarter finals, semi finals and final.
