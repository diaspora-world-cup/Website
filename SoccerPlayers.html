DIASPORA WORLD CUP

International Soccer Tournament

Ottawa, Canada

SOCCER PLAYERS

There are 32 competing teams, 31 of which are countries and 1 of which is the United Nations.

Soccer players sign up to represent the country of their cultural heritage.

Soccer players who do not identify with any of the 31 participating countries can sign up for the United Nations team.
